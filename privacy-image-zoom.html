Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 8, 2025

Image Zoom Extension

This privacy policy describes how the Image Zoom browser extension ("the Extension") handles user information.

Data Collection

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

Specifically, the Extension does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track browsing history
  • Store cookies or local storage data
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Access or modify data on websites beyond its core functionality
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Permissions

The Extension requires certain browser permissions solely to provide its core functionality of zooming and panning images:

  • Active Tab - To interact with images on the current webpage
  • Storage - To save user preferences (zoom level, settings) locally on your device only

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

Functionality

The Extension operates entirely within your browser. It:

  • Detects and enables zoom/pan functionality on images
  • Stores your preferences locally
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not communicate with any external servers

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
